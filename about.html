The project of the team Alpha Canis Majoris for Unihack 2021. Get moving provides a safe and quick way for pedestriand to buy ticket for rides with busses, trains and subways.

Our platform has a dedicated page for providers who want to recieve revenue through our app, which makes for a seamless process.

The users enter a few details about themselves and upon completing the transaction, the get a qr code which can be verified by the company through the verify page.
